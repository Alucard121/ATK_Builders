Years Of Experience

Company Information

ATK is a solid company with a consistent, easily identifiable image since its inception in the year 2004. Drawing on our world-class quality, we deliver pioneering innovations through our well-loved, home-grown brands. We are known in the industry for exclusive items and cater to both the upper and middle market sector.

Today ATK Buildmart is a fashion leader in the retail of sanitary ware, taps, bathtubs, toilets, bidets, basins, bathroom accessories, tiles and other related products in Kerala. Our newest range of products added is built in cabinets and vanities which includes brands that are imported as well as home-grown.

Our portfolio includes iconic brands, like Cera, Parryware, Kohler, Johnson, RAK, Roca International which have established their brand value over the years with their technology and have grown to become experts in water technology. The range of product offering at Build Mart is diverse and includes a broad spectrum of technical products specifically designed for the professional. We also boast a full range of elegant basins, baths and taps that will suit any home and are sure to leave a lasting impression.

Atk Buildmart has commenced its soft lounge on 20th September 2019. ATK Buildmart is currently, The tiles and sanitary wares outlet with the most designs available in Kerala, India including 10,000+ variety of unique designs from almost 47 top quality brands in our 24,000 sq.ft wide outlet

Vision

“To become a trusted brand name in the bathroom and sanitary ware industry in the world.”
